In Store Navigator

The In-Store Navigator Kiosk is an attatchment for grocery shopping carts to assist users with locating items in the store.

Process

Proposal

The first step was to collect data from interviews and online research about how the average shopping experience is for the general population.

Lo-fi Prototype

For the lo-fi prototype, our team used Figma to create a short flow that depicts the main aspects and functions of our interface.
