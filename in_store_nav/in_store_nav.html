In-Store Navigator

The In-Store Navigator Kiosk is an attatchment for grocery shopping carts to assist users with locating items in the store.

Initial Research

Research Findings

Through our intial research, we discovered that millions of Ameicans go to the grocery store everyday, and many of these customers experience frustrations with inconveineces that extend thier grocery shopping time. These frustrations include difficulty finding items, looking for items that are not in stock, and being unable to find an employee for help.
